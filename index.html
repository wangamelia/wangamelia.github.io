about

Hello! I am currently an undergrad at the University of Washington in Seattle, studying Human Centered Design & Engineering. I strive to understand humans, behaviors, and their relationships to technology and how we can design for improvement. For the past two years I have worked under Gary Hsieh in the Prosocial Computing Lab, and I am now also working under Alexis Hiniker in the Information School's User Empowerment Lab. I am currently seeking internship and post-grad opportunities in research and design spaces surrounding behavior, society, and/or user empowerment
